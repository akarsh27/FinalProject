Take the Survey

The purpose of this survey is to measure bias regarding hegemony, meritocracy, and gender roles in the technology sector. This test consists of 18 questions, which should take around 5 minutes to complete, however please go at your own pace.

All responses are anonymous, and results can be viewed either by clicking "View Responses" at the end of the survey to view them in a graph format, or by clicking the results page to view them in a spreadsheet format.

Click "Begin" to start the survey!
